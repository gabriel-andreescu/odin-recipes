Odin Recipes - Tsatsiki

Description

Tzatziki, also known as tarator or cacÄ±k, is a dip, soup, or sauce found in the cuisines of Southeast Europe and the Middle East.

10 mins to prep / 25 mins to cook

Ingredients

  • Olive oil: 27 grams
  • Garlic: 1 clove
  • Nonfat greek yogurt: 227 grams
  • Cucumber: 1/2 medium

Steps

  1. PREPARATION: Peel, seed, and dice cucumber.
  2. Toss cucumber with 1/4 teaspoon each of salt and pepper in a colander and drain 15 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, mince and mash garlic to a paste with 1/4 teaspoon salt, then whisk together with olive oil, yogurt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper.
  4. Squeeze out any excess water from cucumbers with your hands, then stir cucumbers into yogurt mixture. Season with salt.
