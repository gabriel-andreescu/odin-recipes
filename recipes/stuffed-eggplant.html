Odin Recipes - Stuffed Eggplant

Description

Stuffed eggplants are a dish typical of many countries.

10 mins to prep / 50 mins to cook

Ingredients

  • Eggplant: 1 unpeeled
  • Cherry tomatoes: 2 cup
  • Garlic: 1 clove, minced
  • Olive oil: 3 tbsp

Steps

  1. Bake eggplant 30 minutes at 400 degrees F.
  2. Cut in half, drizzle with olive oil, and roast flesh up for another 25 minutes.
  3. Chop tomatoes in the meantime.
  4. Remove eggplant from oven, let cool. Cut out insides and mix with tomatoes, thyme, salt, pepper, garlic. Place mixture back in eggplant halves and serve.
